We would work you through the proceedure of sending money to Family and friends

1. Register for free

There are no fees tied to opening and account, you can sign up for free

2. Choose an amount to send

You are not limited to an amount to send as long as your available balance can cover it. Select an amount to send

3. Add recipient’s bank details

We can send money to anyone in any country, we have done our part by making transfers bortherless. Fill in the recipients full bank details

4. Verify your identity

You would need to verify the transfer with previously defined transfer code.

As long as your available balance can cover the amount to be transfered, it would be deducted and you are good to go.

You don't need to go through any further proceedure. You would be notified about the transfer and that is it..
